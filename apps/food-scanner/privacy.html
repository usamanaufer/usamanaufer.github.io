Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 08, 2026

At Food Scanner, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Camera Access: To scan barcodes, the app requires access to your device's camera. Images captured are processed locally to extract barcode information and are not stored or transmitted to our servers.

Personal Data: We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII).

Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage data to improve the app performance and user experience using tools like Firebase Analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Process barcodes to provide product information.
  • Improve app functionality and user experience.
  • Analyze usage to detect and fix bugs.

3. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services like Firebase and RevenueCat for analytics and subscription management.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions, please contact us at contact@usamanaufer.com.
