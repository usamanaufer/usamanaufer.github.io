Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 08, 2026

At ViralCap, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Data: We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) unless you explicitly provide it (e.g., contacting support).

Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage data to improve the app performance and user experience using tools like Firebase Analytics.

Video Processing: All video processing for captions is performed securely. We do not store your original videos on our servers longer than necessary to process the request.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Provide and maintain our Service.
  • Improve and personalize your experience.
  • Analyze usage to detect and fix bugs.

3. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services like Firebase, RevenueCat, and TikTok SDK for analytics, crash reporting, and subscription management. These services have their own privacy policies.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@usamanaufer.com.
