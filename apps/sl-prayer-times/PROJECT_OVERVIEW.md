# Sri Lanka Prayer Times & Azan

The most accurate, ACJU-verified prayer companion for Muslims in Sri Lanka. This app provides reliable prayer times, Qibla direction, and religious event tracking tailored specifically for the Sri Lankan context.

- **Accurate Prayer Times**: Verified prayer times based on ACJU (All Ceylon Jamiyyathul Ulama) standards for all districts in Sri Lanka.
- **District-Based Localization**: Easily switch between all 25 districts to get precise local timings.
- **Qibla Finder**: A reliable digital compass to help you find the correct direction for prayer from anywhere in the country.
- **Islamic Calendar**: Integrated Hijri calendar showing important Islamic dates and local religious events.
- **Smart Notifications**: Customizable Azan and prayer alerts to ensure you never miss a prayer.
- **Home Screen Widgets**: Quick access to upcoming prayer times directly from your phone's home screen.
- **Premium Features**: Support the developer and unlock enhanced experiences with Pro subscriptions (Monthly & Annual).
- **Offline Reliability**: Local caching of prayer data using Hive database ensures functionality even without an internet connection.
- **Onboarding Experience**: A smooth introduction for new users to set up their location and preferences quickly.
